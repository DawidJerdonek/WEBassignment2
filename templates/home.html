How to play?

{%endblock%} {%block body1%}

A word composed of eight or more letters will be displayed.
You are to input seven words made up of letters contained in the given word.

RULES:

  1. Each word can only be made using the letters contained within the given word.
  2. You cannot use a letter more times than it appears in the given word.
  3. Each word must exist within the dictionary.
  4. Each word must have four letters or more.
  5. There are no duplicate words allowed.
  6. None of the seven words can be the same as the given word.
  7. Character case is not significant
  8. Names will not be counted as words
HAVE FUN :)
